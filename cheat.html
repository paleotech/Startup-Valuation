Valuation Cheat Sheet

This simple calculator provides a quick view of possible pre-money and post-money valuations based on the raise amount and the equity percentage the founders are giving up for that cash.

Enter the raise amount and press the calculate button to display the pre-money and post-money valuations for several common equity percentages.

Recall that dividing your funding raise amount by the percentage equity agreed on with investors results in the post-money valuation of the company.

For example, if you are raising $250,000, and agree on a 10% equity exchange with investors, that means your post-money valuation is $2.5 million.
